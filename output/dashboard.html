Results

This site updates automatically as new results and team lineups come in. Probabilities are model estimates from public data, not guarantees; exact scorelines rarely exceed 15 to 20% even for heavy favourites. Where a starting lineup is known, a match's odds are adjusted for the players missing from it, weighed by EA Sports FC 26 ratings, and the line shows the shift. Fair odds are 1 divided by the model probability and exclude any bookmaker margin, so a wager only carries positive expected value above the price shown; this is information, not betting advice. FIFA rankings are a read-only reference and are not used in the predictions.
